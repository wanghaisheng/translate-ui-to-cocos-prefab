Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI conversion tools

How accurate is the component detection?

Our AI component detection achieves over 94% accuracy across various UI styles and designs. The system is continuously trained on game UI patterns to improve detection quality. For complex or unusual designs, our interactive editor allows you to make adjustments as needed.

Can I customize the generated components?

Yes, our platform includes a comprehensive editor that allows you to adjust component properties, hierarchy, and relationships before exporting to your game engine. You can also save custom presets for future conversions.

What file formats are supported for input?

We support PNG, JPG, WebP, and SVG for image inputs. For code-based inputs, we support HTML, CSS, SVG, and JSON formats. You can also directly paste URLs from supported AI platforms.

How do I integrate with my existing workflow?

We offer plugins for Cocos Creator and Unity that integrate directly with your development environment. For other engines, you can use our universal JSON format and asset export options. We also provide API access for custom integration with your pipeline.

How do I get started?

Simply sign up for a free trial to access all conversion tools. You can immediately start uploading AI designs and converting them to your preferred game engine format. Our guided workflow will help you through each step of the process.
