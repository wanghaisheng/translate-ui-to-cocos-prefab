Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI conversion tools

What AI platforms are supported?

We support all major AI art generators including Midjourney, Ideogram, ChatGPT, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion. You can also upload designs from any custom AI source.

How accurate is the component detection?

Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy in detecting UI components across different AI art styles. The detection is optimized for each AI platform's unique characteristics.

Which game engines are supported?

We currently support Cocos Creator 3.x, Unity (UGUI), and Unreal Engine (UMG). More engines will be added based on user demand.

Yes, all generated prefabs are fully customizable. You can adjust properties, add functionality, and modify the hierarchy to suit your specific needs.
